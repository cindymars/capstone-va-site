About Me

How can a virtual assistant help your business?

Growing your business can be a challenge, especially when you need to deal with many repetitive or administrative tasks. A Virtual Assistant can do all that and more.

Virtual Assistants can offer a variety of services: copywriting and proofreading, admin and calendar management, graphic design, web maintenance, social media management, research, and more.

Hello, my name is Cindy Martinez. I have worked for over five years in sales operations, ensuring that the frontline sales teams are able to sell more efficiently and effectively. As such, I have experience launching and managing four major and three minor range change events at a large retailer by developing detailed planograms and implementing strategic product placement to act on business development initiatives, market trends, and changing availability of products, while promoting business commercial priorities and volume commitments.

This required a lot of knowledge in a variety of topics such as data analytics, visual merchandising, and project management. As your Virtual Assistant, I can leverage my prior experiences to assist your business with strategy, tech integration, and performance analytics.
